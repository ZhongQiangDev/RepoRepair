### 1. Identify the Wrong Behavior Involved in `GitHub Problem Description`

The wrong behavior described is that after upgrading from version 6.1 to 7.0, the tile loading behavior for layers within a `LayerGroup` changed. In version 6.1, tile requests were sent concurrently, allowing imagery to load quickly. However, in version 7.0, tile requests are sent sequentially, leading to slower imagery load times.

### 2. Analyze the Cause Behind the Wrong Behavior

The cause of this behavior likely lies in changes to how tile requests are prioritized and managed within the `LayerGroup` and related classes. Specifically, the way tiles are queued and loaded in the `TileQueue` class may have been altered, leading to sequential rather than concurrent loading.
