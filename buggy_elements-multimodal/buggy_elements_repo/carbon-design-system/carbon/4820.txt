- **Contrast Issues**: The contrast issue suggests that the colors used for the text and background do not meet the required contrast ratio. This could be due to hardcoded colors or styles that do not account for different states (e.g., disabled, invalid).

- **Validation State Issues**: The validation state issue indicates that the component does not properly handle or display error messages when the form is invalid. This could be due to missing or incorrect logic for displaying the `invalidText` or other validation-related attributes.
