### 1. Identify the Wrong Behavior Involved in `GitHub Problem Description`

The GitHub problem description identifies two main issues:

1. **Contrast Issue**: The contrast of the "Skip to Main" link does not meet the WCAG AA requirements.
2. **WAI-ARIA Role Issue**: The `menuitem` role is not contained within a valid parent element with one of the following WAI-ARIA roles: `group`, `menu`, or `menubar`.

### 2. Analyze the Cause Behind the Wrong Behavior

- **Contrast Issue**: This issue likely arises from the styling of the "Skip to Main" link. The link's text color and background color do not provide sufficient contrast, leading to a contrast ratio that fails to meet the WCAG AA requirements.
  
- **WAI-ARIA Role Issue**: This issue occurs because the `menuitem` elements are not properly nested within a parent element that has the appropriate WAI-ARIA role (`group`, `menu`, or `menubar`). This can be due to missing or incorrect role attributes in the parent elements of the `menuitem` elements.
