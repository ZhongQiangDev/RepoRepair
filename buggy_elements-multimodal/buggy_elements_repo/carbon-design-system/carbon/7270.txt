### 1. Identify the Wrong Behavior Involved in `GitHub Problem Description`

The wrong behavior described is that when a `ref` is bound to a `Dropdown` or `Multiselect` component, the `ref` always returns `null` or `undefined`. This indicates that the `ref` is not being correctly forwarded to the underlying DOM element or the component itself.

### 2. Analyze the Cause Behind the Wrong Behavior

The issue likely stems from a problem with `forwardRef` or ref forwarding in the `Dropdown` and `Multiselect` components. When a `ref` is passed to a component, it should be forwarded to the underlying DOM element or another component that needs it. If the `ref` is not being forwarded correctly, it will result in `null` or `undefined` when accessed.
