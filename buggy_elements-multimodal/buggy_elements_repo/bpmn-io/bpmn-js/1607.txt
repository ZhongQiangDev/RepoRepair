### 1. Identify the Wrong Behavior Involved in `GitHub Problem Description`

The wrong behavior described is that when a group is created inside a collapsed sub-process and the diagram is saved, upon re-importing the diagram, a warning appears stating `unresolved reference <CategoryValue_01k5toe>`. This indicates that the BPMN export process is not correctly handling the group elements within collapsed sub-processes, leading to unresolved references during re-import.

### 2. Analyze the Cause Behind the Wrong Behavior

The cause of this issue likely stems from how the BPMN exporter handles the creation and management of `bpmn:CategoryValue` elements when groups are added inside collapsed sub-processes. When a group is created, a corresponding `bpmn:CategoryValue` is generated and linked to the group. However, when the sub-process is collapsed, the exporter might not properly serialize this relationship, leading to the `CategoryValue` being lost or improperly referenced in the exported BPMN file. Upon re-import, the system cannot resolve the reference to the missing `CategoryValue`, resulting in the warning.
