## Bug Analysis

The issue is requesting to include emulated screen dimensions and deviceScaleFactor in the report meta block, specifically in the devices tooltip. Currently, the tooltip shows information about CPU/Memory power, but doesn't include screen dimensions or device scale factor.

Looking at the code, the relevant section is in the `_renderMetaBlock` method of the `ReportRenderer` class. This method creates the meta information displayed in the footer of the report, including the devices tooltip.

The fix needs to add the screen dimensions and deviceScaleFactor to the tooltip text for the devices item in the meta block.

## Solution

I need to modify the `_renderMetaBlock` method to include the screen dimensions and deviceScaleFactor in the tooltip for the devices item. The information should be available in the `report.configSettings` object, which is already being used to get other emulation descriptions.
