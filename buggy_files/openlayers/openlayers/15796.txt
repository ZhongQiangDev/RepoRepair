### 1. Identify the Wrong Behavior Involved in `GitHub Problem Description`

The wrong behavior described is that `VectorLayer` with `declutter` enabled and `opacity` set to less than 1 does not clear the previous frame during rendering. This results in a visual artifact where the previous frame's content remains visible, leading to a cluttered appearance.

### 2. Analyze the Cause Behind the Wrong Behavior

The issue likely stems from how the `VectorLayer` handles rendering when both `declutter` and `opacity` are applied. When `declutter` is enabled, the layer attempts to avoid overlapping labels and symbols. However, when `opacity` is less than 1, the layer may not properly clear the canvas between renders, causing remnants of the previous frame to persist. This could be due to a problem in the rendering pipeline where the canvas is not cleared or the blending mode is not correctly set to handle semi-transparent layers.
