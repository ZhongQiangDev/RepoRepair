### 1. Identify the Wrong Behavior Involved in `GitHub Problem Description`

The wrong behavior described is that the cancel search icon button (the "X" button inside the search input field) has poor color contrast when the search input field has a `skyblue` background color due to Chrome's autofill feature. This makes the icon difficult to see and interact with.

### 2. Analyze the Cause Behind the Wrong Behavior

The cause of this issue is likely related to the styling of the cancel search icon button. The button's color is not dynamically adjusting to maintain sufficient contrast when the background color changes due to Chrome's autofill feature. This could be due to hardcoded colors or a lack of CSS rules that adapt to different background colors.
