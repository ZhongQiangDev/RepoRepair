### 1. Identify the Wrong Behavior Involved in GitHub Problem Description

The wrong behavior described is that when a child lane is removed from a parent lane in a BPMN diagram, the diagram becomes deformed, and sometimes it becomes impossible to continue editing the diagram. This deformation likely involves incorrect resizing or repositioning of the remaining lanes and possibly other related elements.

### 2. Analyze the Cause Behind the Wrong Behavior

The cause of this behavior could be related to how the system handles the deletion of a lane and the subsequent adjustment of the remaining lanes. Specifically, the issue might stem from:

- Incorrect calculation of new bounds for the remaining lanes after a lane is removed.
- Failure to properly update the positions and sizes of related elements (e.g., other lanes, flow nodes, connections).
- Lack of proper constraints or rules for maintaining the integrity of the diagram structure during lane deletion.
