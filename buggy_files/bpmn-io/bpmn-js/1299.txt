### 1. Identify the Wrong Behavior Involved in `GitHub Problem Description`

The GitHub problem description outlines two main issues:

1. **Label Resizing Issue**: When completing direct editing with an empty label, the label target resizes to minimum bounds, causing the label to become smaller than normal or disappear.
2. **Element Resizing Issue**: After selecting an event or gateway, the shapes of the elements change quickly and become smaller, and in some cases, gateways disappear.

### 2. Analyze the Cause Behind the Wrong Behavior

#### Label Resizing Issue:
- The issue likely stems from how the label's bounds are calculated and updated when the label text is empty. The system may not handle empty labels correctly, leading to the label being resized to its minimum bounds.

#### Element Resizing Issue:
- The problem may be related to how the system handles the selection and rendering of events and gateways. When an event or gateway is selected, the system might be incorrectly updating the element's bounds or visual properties, causing the elements to resize or disappear.
