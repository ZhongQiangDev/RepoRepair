### 1. Identify the Wrong Behavior Involved in `GitHub Problem Description`

The wrong behavior is that when the user drags the mouse outside the modal while trimming the sound, the modal unexpectedly closes, causing the user to lose their work.

### 2. Analyze the Cause Behind the Wrong Behavior

The issue likely arises because the modal is configured to close when the mouse is clicked outside of it. This behavior is probably triggered by the `onRequestClose` function in the `ModalComponent`. When the user drags the mouse outside the modal while trimming, it may be interpreted as a click outside the modal, causing it to close.
