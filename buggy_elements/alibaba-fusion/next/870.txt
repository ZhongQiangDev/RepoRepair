### 1. Identify the Wrong Behavior Involved in `GitHub Problem Description`

#### Overlay Issue:
- **Wrong Behavior**: The datepicker popup appears in the wrong position after scrolling down while the datepicker is still in the viewport.
- **Steps to Reproduce**: Click the datepicker, scroll down a little bit, and then click the datepicker again.

#### NumberPicker Issue:
- **Wrong Behavior**: The `innerBefore` style is incorrect and does not support `innerAfter`.
- **Steps to Reproduce**: The images show that the `innerBefore` content is styled incorrectly and there is no support for `innerAfter`.

### 2. Analyze the Cause Behind the Wrong Behavior

#### Overlay Issue:
- **Possible Cause**: The positioning logic for the overlay might not be correctly accounting for the scroll position of the page or the container. The overlay's position is likely being calculated based on the initial position of the trigger element, but it does not adjust for the scroll offset when the page is scrolled.

#### NumberPicker Issue:
- **Possible Cause**: The `NumberPicker` component might not be properly handling the `innerBefore` and `innerAfter` props. The styles for `innerBefore` might be incorrect, and the component might not be rendering `innerAfter` at all.
