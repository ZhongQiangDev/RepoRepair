### 1. Identify the Wrong Behavior Involved in `GitHub Problem Description`

The wrong behavior described is that Jetpack monthly plans are showing an error message indicating they will expire in a month, which is causing unnecessary anxiety for users. The message suggests adding a credit card for auto-renewal, but this might not be necessary or relevant for all users, especially those using credits for their purchases.

### 2. Analyze the Cause Behind the Wrong Behavior

The cause of this issue likely stems from the logic that determines when to display the error message about the plan expiring. The message is probably being triggered based on the plan's expiration date and the absence of a billable payment method. However, for users who are using credits, this message is misleading because their plan does not require a credit card for auto-renewal.
