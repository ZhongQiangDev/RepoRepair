### 1. Identify the Wrong Behavior Involved in `GitHub Problem Description`

The wrong behavior described is that when a user opens a `ComboBox` and then clicks on the chevron to open another `ComboBox`, the previously opened `ComboBox` does not dismiss. This issue is specific to `ComboBox` components and does not occur with `Dropdown` components.

### 2. Analyze the Cause Behind the Wrong Behavior

The cause of this issue likely lies in the way the `ComboBox` component manages its open state and how it handles clicks outside of its own boundaries. Specifically, the `ComboBox` component may not be properly detecting clicks outside of itself to close the dropdown menu. This could be due to a missing or incorrect implementation of an outside click detection mechanism.
