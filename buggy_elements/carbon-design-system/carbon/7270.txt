### 1. Identify the Wrong Behavior Involved in `GitHub Problem Description`

The wrong behavior described is that binding a `ref` to a `Dropdown` or `Multiselect` component results in the `ref` being `undefined` or `null`. This indicates that the `ref` is not being correctly forwarded to the underlying DOM element or component.

### 2. Analyze the Cause Behind the Wrong Behavior

The issue likely stems from a problem with `forwardRef` implementation in the `Dropdown` or `Multiselect` components. When a `ref` is passed to a component, it should be forwarded to the underlying DOM element or another component that needs it. If the `ref` is `undefined`, it suggests that the `forwardRef` mechanism is not properly set up or is being overridden somewhere in the component hierarchy.
