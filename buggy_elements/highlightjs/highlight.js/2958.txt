This issue affects the proper syntax highlighting of XML tags, causing them to appear unformatted or improperly formatted.

### 2. Analyze the Cause Behind the Wrong Behavior

The cause of this issue likely lies in how the syntax highlighting library handles namespace prefixes in XML tags. Specifically, the regular expressions or rules used to identify and highlight XML tags may not be correctly accounting for single-letter namespace prefixes. This could be due to a change in the regex patterns or the logic that processes these tags.
